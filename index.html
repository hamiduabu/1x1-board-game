one X one

Turn Based Board Game

Instructions

The objective is simple:

  • You start with a basic weapon with 10 Attack Points(AP)
  • Four extra weapons are randomly distributed on the board.
  • Replace your weapon with one on the board with the highest potential AP
  • Pursue your opponent to their adjacent square before they upgrade theirs.
  • Smash 'em!!!
  • Move around the board with your keyboard's arrow keys
  • You have 3 moves per turn.
  • You cannot move outside the board or through barriers
  • Available squares are highlighted in green.
